Information We Collect

In order to provide you with our services, we require certain data. In our databases we store your name, netID, department, and class year. Additionally, we store your buddy list and any chat rooms that you have joined. No information is stored regarding conversations. Our server automatically logs the last ten messages you send to any contact and no more, so we never have more than a brief segment of any of your conversations stored on our servers.
